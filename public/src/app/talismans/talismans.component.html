Talismans

Talismans is a calm, flow-inducing, procedurally-generated, multiple-puzzles, jigsaw puzzle game based on hexagons and triangles and ... grief. You find the pieces in containers piled up in a typical level, no enemies, no deaths, just lots of things to break, and lots of things to put back in its place.
