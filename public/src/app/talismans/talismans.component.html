Talismans

Talismans is a calm, flow-inducing, procedurally-generated, jigsaw puzzle game based on hexagons and triangles. You find puzzle pieces scattered around a Zelda-like dungeon, no enemies, no deaths. Then, each piece needs to find its home on one of many puzzle boards. Lots of discovery and putting things back in their place, when the outside world rages on.

I've been developing this game since August 2020.

Technologies

Written in C++, Unreal Engine, Procedurally-Generated, Blueprint Materials
