Blender Experience

Other than the Obligatory Tutorial Donut, I've used Blender to work on my current project, Talismans. The models aren't complex, but I did need to be very precise with them. My levels are procedurally generated, so I built the foundation of a kit that I can continue to iterate on.

Puzzle pieces had to be precise, so they can procedurally be made and still fit together in a consistent manner.

This all forced me to understand the importance of controlling your UV Maps.
