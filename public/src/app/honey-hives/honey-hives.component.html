Honey Hives

Honey Hives is a kind of Hexagon Sodoku using tiles that you can maybe spin or maybe move. The key to figuring out these puzzles is to start at the Equal Signs (=), for if they create a contradiction, your beautiful flowers will not bloom.

I am building this game to expand my knowledge of C# and the Unity engine, and to learn the differences and similiarites between the popular engines. My understanding of Unreal helped to make learning Unity a fairly quick exercise.
